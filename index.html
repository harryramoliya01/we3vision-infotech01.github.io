Our Treks

We offer a wide range of treks that cover a wide range of experience levels. Check out some examples below, and feel free to get in touch if you have any questions or would like to book.

  • Upper Mustang Trekking

    The last forbidden kingdom Mustang is also known as mystique valley and "mini Tibet of Nepal"

  • Langtang Gosainkunda Trekking

    Langtang Gosainkunda Lake Trekking combines the isolated and stunning valley of Langtang with holy high mountain lakes such as Gosainkunda and Bhairabkunda.

  • Nar Phu Valley Trekking

    Nar Phu Valley Trekking is the combination between both Nar and Phu village which is located isolated part of Annapurna region.

  • Langtang Village Trekking

    Langtang Valley Trekking is one of the nearest trekking destination from Kathmandu located in central north part of Nepal which combines the natural beauty and cultural diversity.

  • Manaslu Larke Pass

    Manaslu trekking considered as most challenging, untouched and scenic trekking trail in Midwestern part of Nepal which rewards you adventure journey with an unparalleled alpine experience.

  • Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking

    Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking is newly opened trekking route in Langtang region located in cenral nothern part of Nepal.
